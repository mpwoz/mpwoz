Multisnake

Another Node application, it's a remake of the classic arcade game, Snake. The twist is that it supports multiple players, each taking control of their own snake and competing for berries! My first experience with the wonderful Coffeescript as well as more Node practice. May run a little slow due to hosting limitations.
